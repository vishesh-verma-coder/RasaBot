Group Overview

Expertise & Solution Centre (ESC) is the CSO expertise center & integrator reference point, supplementing FBO and IBO. It is responsible for providing our customers with end-to-end integration of their solutions and services. ESC team in India MSC supports UCC, Voice, Video, Contact Center and Security Operation and provides expertise in product & process development working with OCD, OAB, OCB and UCC business units while ensuring overall consistency. India MSC ESC will also be responsible for incubation of new services, such as SDN unified digital desk and Technical Skill Center. The function is headed by Sanchita Biswas.
